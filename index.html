FAQ

How much are tickets and when can I buy them?

Tickets are $2 and are assigned seating. Tickets will go on sale March 10th & 11th before and after school in the entrance hallway.

What do I need to do for my child's costume?

We will let you know what clothes will be needed for individual costumes as we get closer. Mostly we will need clothes that will fit easily under their costumes so they can change with other kids without feeling uncomfortable.

When are cast pictures and head shots for the playbill?

We are looking at the second weekend in February. Friday and Saturday. As soon as we know the times, we will let you know.

How much are T-shirts and when will we get them?

T-shirts are included in the price of the musical and will be handed out as soon as they are printed.
